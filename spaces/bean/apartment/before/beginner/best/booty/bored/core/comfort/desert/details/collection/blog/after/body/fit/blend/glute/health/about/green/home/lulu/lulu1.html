Fitness Blender's 4 Week
Vegetarian Meal Plan and Nutrition Guide

By Daniel Segars & Keli Segars

Price $24.99

Built with registered dietitians and nutritionists, this
healthy, clean-eating Vegetarian Meal Plan details 3
meals, and 3 snacks, everyday for four weeks.
There is a corresponding grocery list for each week,
and there are 5 different calorie allotments to
choose from so that you can customize this plan to
meet your exact goals(weight loss, gain, or
maintenance). Includes suggestions for modifying
for food allergies, intolerance, and availability, as
well as food costs, cooking for a family, and more
Couple this 4 Week Meal Plan with our 8 Week Fat
Loss program for foolproof results. Includes
modification for vegetarians who do not consume
eggs; our Vegan Meal Plan may also be of interest
for a completely animal-product free meal plan.

This ebook has a lot of information about how to eat right. And for the
most part it's really helpful! It also give some very nice ideas for snacks
and small meals. Howevery, this book is not well thought out in terms of
being a meal plan that one can follow as is. it does not take leftovers
into consideration (what am i going to do with 2/3 of an avocado when I
won't be using it for another 3 days?..) and generally provides very
bare-bone recipes without much flavor or knowledge behind the
cooking process (bean soup that is made by adding water and some
spices to cooked beans - just no). It will fit People who don't particularly
like cooking or who like putting raw produce on a plate and eating it all
seperately - there is absolutely nothing wrong with that, but my
stomach specifically needs some well-balanced, cooked meals at least
once a week. and this guide is of no help.
