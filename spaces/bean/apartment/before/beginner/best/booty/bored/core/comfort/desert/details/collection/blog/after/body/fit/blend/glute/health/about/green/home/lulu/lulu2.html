Fitness Blender's 4 Week Vegan
Meal Plan and Nutrition Guide

By Daniel Segars & Kelli Segars

Price: $24.99

Built with registered dietitians and nutritionists, this
healthy, clean-eating Vegan Meal Plan details 3
meals, and 3 snacks, every day for four weeks
There is a corresponding grocery list for each week,
and there are 5 different calorie allotments to
choose from so that you can customize this plan to
meet your exact goals (weight loss, gain, or
maintenance). Includes suggestions for modifying
for food allergies, intolerance, and availability, as
well as food costs, cooking for a family, and more.
Couple this 4 Week Meal Plan with our 8 Week Fat
Loss Programs for foolproof results.

By Bests J Westlund

I love a lot about this meal plan! It's laid out fantastically and I found it
easy to adapt to a family of 4. The meals are straight forward and
contain a good variety. I was a little disapointed that it's not fully
vegan since it has honey and greek yogurt as part of the meal plan.
Honey is derived form bees in a way that is detrimental to the bees so
it's a little jarring to have to alter the meal plan several times to
accomodate for this. Greek yogurt is milk derived and horrendously
terrible for the enviroment. Beside the non-vegan flaws, it's still an
amazing meal plan and I would recommend it to anyome looking for a
meal plan to get to know what healthy eating looks like. if this were
ever adapted, I would also recommend less use tofu. Soy is a
common allergy and also not good for the enviroment which is a big
deal to many vegans. The health aspects of consuming large quantities
of soy also questionable. I adapted to using beans for a lot more of
the recipes.
