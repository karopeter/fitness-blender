FB Burn - Round 2 - Smart HIIT & Strength
Program to Get Fit Quick

Overview
FB Burn Round 2 is here! This 4 Week Program uses a smart combination of strength training, high intensity
interval trainig (HIIT)m functional training, Pilates, cardio, stretching and more. There are no repeat workouts in
this entire program; you'll be doing a different workout video every single day. Workout video lengths range
between 33-45 minutes (38 minutes on average), 5 days a week with an optional 6th day recovery workout
that focuses on flexibility.

You can complete this program at any pace you like, as many times as you like (at no extra charge). Our
updated online workout calendar lets you customize the program's pace to work for your needs, and you'll get
the satisfication of tracking your progress by checking "Workout Complete" after each day's workout!

Similar to our very pooular 4 Week FB Sweat, this new FB Burn Program provides you with an optional Extra
Credit Challenge, 3-4 times a week. that allows you to bump up the length and intensity of your training
session, if you have the extra time and energy.

This Program is similar in concept to FB Burn Round 1, but uses differnt workout videos and challenges. Each
round is a standalone program; you can dp these program in any order.

This program is excellent for fat and weight loss, building lean muscle, drastic improvements in body tone,
endurance, strength, & flexibility gains. We walk you through the program every step of th way and include a
workout & clean eating guide to give you the essential on how to properly nourish yourself during this program
(please note, this is a general and not a meal plan).

These workout are intense but the vast majority of fitness levels have been able to partake in these programs
as you are encpuraged to go at your own pace and modify whenever necessary - however, this is not
a beginner workout program.

Use #FBburn on social media to track your workout sessions, share your progress, find motivation, and
connect with people around the world who are following this program.
