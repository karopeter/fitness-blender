FB Reach -Stretching, Yoga, & Pilates
Program for Flexibility & Total Body Toning

Overview
Pilated, yoga, and stretching come together to create an easy-on-the-bosy blend ideal for increasing flexibility,
tone, and range of motion. The heavy emphasis on stretching is highly effective for increasing flexibility, and
the Pilates and yoga blends are great for toning the abs, butt, and thighs, as well as the upper body. The
training styles in this program are also excellent for increasing mind-body awareness and control over your own
body. The FB Reach workout are 5 days a week, with an optional! 6th day, and each day's workout session
requires roughly 20-30 minutes. Combine this flexibility program with any other Fitness Blender program to
accelerate your flexibility gains, improve core strength, and tone up all over. This is a low impact plan that is
suitable for most fitness levels.

We encourage you to use #FBreach on social media to track your workout sessions, share your progress, find
motivation, and connect with people arou nd the world who are following this program.
