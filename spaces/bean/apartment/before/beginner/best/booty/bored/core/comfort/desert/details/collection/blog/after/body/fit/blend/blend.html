4 Week FB Blend - Burn Fat, Build Muscle,
Tine; 35 or 55 Minutes a Day

Overview

FB Blend is a fun, challenging, and flexible workout plan; you get to choose the length of your workouts each
day, roughly 35 or 55 minutes a day, depending on your own schedule and energy levels. Each day of this
program offers a unique workout - you'll never have to do the same vidoe twice. It's a smart combination of
HIIT, strength training, functional movement, pilates,supersets,cardio,polymetrics,stretching,yoga and
more.All you need is dumbbells and even if you don't have dumbbells, you can till get in a great workout using
your own bodyweight.

Because you pick the length of your workout each day, this program is essentially two of our most popular
programs in one; FB30(our 30 minute program) and FBFit(our 50 minute program).If you love FB Sweat,
or FB Burn, you will love FB Blend becuase of the shared flexibility in actual workout length.

The strategic combination of HIIT and strength training in this total body program is excellent for burning fat,
building lean muscle, and becoming overall more physically healthy and capable. These are tried and true,
scientifically-backed training methods, The workouts will challenge your muscular strength, cardiovascular
enudrance, coordination, speed,proproception, balance, and flexibility. These workouts can absolutely make
you look great, but we want you to also feel great. Our aim is to guide you to move well, and live well. We want
you to feel strong and capable, energetic and able to live easily in your own body.

Workout are 5 days a week + an optional 6th recovery day workout but it's important to note that our online
workout calendar allows you to complete these workouts at any pace you like. Additionally, our programs can
be reused as many time as you like, at no extra charge whatsoever.

FB Blend workouts are between 32-45 minutes, averaging out to 38 minutes/day.Each day offers an Extra
Credit Challenge, in case you've got the extra-time and energy to push yourself a little harder (this is what
allows you the option to bump you workout length up to ~55 minutes). These Extra Credit Challenges are
always complimentary of, but a twist on, the main workout, in order to push you in varied, dynamic ways and
help you step up both the challenge, intensity, length and diversity of the workouts.

We include a workout and clean eating guide to give you the essentials on how to properly nourish yourself
during the program(Please not, this is a general guide and not a meal plan).

Difficulty level: We always encourage each person to listen to their own body's message and move at their
own pace. With that said; these workouts are intense and start out at difficulty levels 3-5 from Day 1. This is not
a Low Imoact program, thouhg most of though most of the workouts (not all) provide low impact alternatives. Exercisers will
need to use thier own discretion in manipulating the scheduled workout to be appropriate for their personal
fitness levels and abilities. Exercisers are encouraged to tkae liberties in modifying the exercises and this
program wo work for their needs. Listento and respect your body!
