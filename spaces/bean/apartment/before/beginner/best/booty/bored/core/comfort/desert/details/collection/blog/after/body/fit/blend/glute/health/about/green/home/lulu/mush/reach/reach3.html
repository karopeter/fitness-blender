FB 30 - 8 Week Fat Loss For Busy People:
Lose Weight, Tone Up, Build Lean Muscle

Overview
Combine fat-blasting HIIT with metabolism-boosting strength to bring incredible results quickly
with workouts that are 30 minutes or less!

This challenging home workout program only requires dumbbells - and uses HIIT, cardio, strength training,
circuit training, supersets, plyometrics, Pilates, and yoga come together to create the ideal workout program.
Many people who compelete these programs see weight loss, reduced body fat, drastic improvements in body
tone, endurance, strength, & flexibility gains.

Actual workout length vary, butr average out to 30 minutes a day, five days a week (with an optional recovery
workout on the 6th day). 30 Minutes is 1/48th of your day, so no more excuses!

These workouts are challenging, but the vast majority of fitness levels have been able to partake in these
programs as you are encouraged to go at your own pace - we have also included beginner alternatives in the
first 2 weeks if the regular workouts are initially too difficult.

Us #FB30 on social media to track your workout sessions, share your progress, find motivation, and connect
with people around the world who are following this program.
