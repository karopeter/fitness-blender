FB Fit - Round 2 - 8 Week Fat Loss Program
to Lose Weight, Build Lean Muscle & Tone Up

Overview
Welcome to FBFit Round 2; all new workouts and all new challenges. This program offers daily guidance in the
form of a clear, easy-to-follow workout plan using our full length workout videos, filled with daily helpful
tips, while the calendar tracks your progress through he challenging and highly effective cardio, strength
training, HIIT , ploymetric, Pilates, circuit training, stretching yoga, balance & agility training workouts, to help
you get fit as fast as possible.

Once you buy them, these programs are yours to keep and they may be repeated as many times as you
like. Many people who complete these programs see weight loss and increased lean muscle, as well as
reduced body fat, drastic improvements in body tone, endurance, strength, & flexibility gains. Workouts are 5
days a week, with an optional 6th day of stretching or yoga & the workout sessions average out to roughly
50 minutes each (with the exception of a couple of 1000 calorie workout days). This workout programs includes
a guide to clean eating that gives you a general but incredibly effective rundown on how to eat during this
program for better health & faster results (please note, this is a general guide and not a meal plan). Each day
you get a calorie burn estimate as well as frequent opportunities for bonus workout challenges to see results
even faster. The vast majority of fitness levels have been able to partake in these programs as you are
encouraged to go at your own pace - we have also included beginner alternatives in the first 2 weeks if the
regular workouts are initially too difficult. All you need is dumbbells.

We encourage you to use #FBfit on social media to track your workout sessions, share your progress, find
motivation, and connect with people around the world who are following this program.
