Apartment-friendly workouts that pack a
punch: workout videos for apartments & small
spaces

W hen I was a (completely & totally broke) college student and for a few years after, I lived in
small apartments. I was still mostly doing gym workouts at that point, but every now and again I
would tackle a workout in my apartment. That lasted until I dropped my rent off at the apartment manager's
office, and they asked, "Do you dance around at night...or something? Because the neighbors downstairs are
wondering waht it is you're doing up there."

It's important to be considerate of your neigbors. You never know who might be need to wak up early for
work or who might be sleeping before they tackle a night shift or have a new baby or just want to get some
quiet time without the pounding of someone's feet overhead. That's fair. But it also seems a fair objective to
want to be able to work out in your won living space - it's a seriously convenient, excuse-busting, money-
saving option. In an effort to keep both you and your downstairs neigbors happy, I've bult a list of apartment
friendly workout videos that take into consideration the impact of landing and the amount of space you might
have available. These same routines may be great choices for hotel room workouts, too!

We also have over 500 workout videos that could also be easily modified to work for small spaces and
neighbors below (never hesitate to modify an exercise to make it work for your needs).

Strength Training
Lifting is a great option for apartment workouts because they don't require any jumping; you can get in a great
workout that builds muscle and makes you sweat, with almost no impact. All you need is a set of dumbbells or
resistance bands and an optional exercise mat. I favor dumbbells, but resistance bands are cheaper and take
up even less space. In terms of dumbbells, these adjustable PowerBlocks are spendy, but are well built and
take up very little room (because of the way they fit together, the entire set has the footprint of a single large
dumbbell).

◇ Lower body focus: 40 Minutes Build a Booty Workout - Strength and Pilates Workotut for Butt and Thighs

◇ Upper body focus: 35 Minute Upper Body and Abs Workout - Compound Upper Body Workout for Strength
and Coordination

◇ Core & cardio focus: 39 Minute Low impact Cardio and Abs Workout with Warm Up and Cool Down

Need something shorter? Here are 10 minute versions of all of the same muscle groups:

◇ lower body focus: 10 Minute Butt and Thigh Workout - No Equipment Lower Body Workout
◇ Upper body focus: Fun Upper Body Workout + A Message About Goal Setting
◇ Core focus: 10 Minute Abs Workout - At Home Abs Workout with No Equipment

Apartment-Friendly Cardio Workouts
Cardio workouts may seem like an extra challenging, but the trick is to choose low impact cardio workouts that
don't involve jumping. That way, you can work up a good sweat without botheriung neighbors. Here are a few
options that you can choose from, based on how much time you might have to commit to exercise today:

◇ 10 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners - Feel Good Cardio
◇ 26 Minute Low Impact Beginner Cardio Workout - Feel Good Recovery Cardio
◇ 55 Minute Brutal Low Impact Workout - Advanced Functional Strength, Balance and Core Workout

Pilates Workouts
Pilates is a great way to get in a quiet workout that also uses a small workout space. None of these workouts
involve jumping, but they're all great for increasing range of mtion, flexibility, and overall body awareness.

◇ 10 Minutes Abs Workout - Pilates Abs Burner
◇ 20 Minute Pilates Workout: Pilates Butt and Thigh Workout
◇ 35 Minute Abs and Lower Body Pilates Workout for Beginners
◇ 62 Minute Pilates Abs, and Thigh Workout - Pilates Workout for Lower Body & Core

Yoga / Stretching
Always important, yoga and/or stretching can be done nearly anywhere without botheing anyone else. These
workouts are slow, calm, and relaxing -and can be great way to start your day, or end it. All of these also feel
great on muscles that are sore from previous workouts. Whether you're looking to gain flexibility or just mellow
out, these stretching workouts won't bother your neighbors and they're easy workouts to do in hotel rooms as
well.

◇ 9 Minute Chill Out! Stretching, Pilates, Yoga Workout for Flexibility and Calm
◇ 18 Minute Lower Back Stretches for stiff Sore Muscles
◇ 3 Day Flexibility Challenge Day 1: Fluid Full Body Stretches for Flexibility & Stress Relief

Hopefully you'll find a few workouts and training styles on the list that will allow you to fire up your muscles and
your lungs, without getting an eviction notice.
